Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Apex Signals via Discord, we may collect:

  • Discord User ID and username — for role management and subscription tracking
  • Payment information — processed by NOWPayments (we never see your wallet keys)
  • Usage data — commands used, subscription start/end dates
  • Trade results — anonymized signal performance data for ML training

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To grant and manage Discord role access based on subscription
  • To send subscription-related DMs (expiry warnings, receipts)
  • To improve signal accuracy via ML model training (anonymized)
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on the server running the bot. We do not use third-party analytics services. Payment data is handled exclusively by NOWPayments under their privacy policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Payment processors (NOWPayments, Whop) as necessary to complete transactions
  • When required by law

5. Data Retention

Subscription and payment records are retained for accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data by opening a support ticket.

6. Discord

By using our Discord server, you also agree to Discord's Privacy Policy (discord.com/privacy). We cannot control data processed by Discord itself.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing DMs by contacting support

8. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify members of significant changes via Discord announcements.

9. Contact

For privacy requests, open a ticket in our Discord server or email us at the address in the server's info channel.
